Our Services

See our services below to find out how we can help your business enhance its security posture:



Security Controls Framework

If you struggle to translate external standards or customer security questionnaires into actionable steps, we can help.

We work with you to map external requirements to your internal technical controls, creating a structured control framework that guides implementation and provides consistent, evidence-backed responses to auditors. This allows your team to focus on running and developing the business rather than repeatedly interpreting compliance requirements.

Expert at Hand

Have a security expert available whenever you need guidance.

Gain confidence in your risk assessments, security engineering decisions, and compliance strategies, knowing you have support to make informed choices and avoid costly mistakes.

Security Review

Comprehensive assessments of your controls, systems, and products, including application and infrastructure threat modeling and cloud configuration review.

We provide a tailored report highlighting your security posture and helping you focus investments where they matter most. Each assessment can be conducted individually or as part of a series, allowing you to start small and see results quickly.


Who We Are


Solais Security

Solais Security is a boutique security consultancy founded by Kevin Quill, based in the Republic of Ireland. We provide pragmatic security solutions and guidance for start-ups and SMEs, focusing on threat modelling, cloud architecture, control design, and developing control frameworks that align external compliance requirements with internal technologies and working practices.

Specific services include:

  • Threat Modelling & Risk Assessments
  • Cloud Security Architecture (AWS,GCP)
  • Security Control Design (Cloud, IAM, Vulnerability Managment, etc..)
  • Aligning Compliance Frameworks (ISO 27001, PCI, etc.) with your technical controls and processes
  • General Security Consultancy Retainer - Have a security expert at hand when needed

The Founder

  • Communicates effectively across audiences, from engineers to senior leadership, tailoring technical and risk insights to drive alignment and investment
  • Builds bridges between Security and Engineering, fostering trust and collaboration to deliver outcomes with measurable business value
