Services

See below to find out how I can help your business enhance its security posture:



Baseline Security Assessment

SMB Non-Technical
Before recommending what to fix, we need to understand what you have.

This assessment documents your current security posture: the systems you rely on, what protections are already in place, and where the gaps are. You get an honest starting point with findings in plain language, not generic IT terminology.

Security Strategy Development

SMB Non-Technical
A practical roadmap for closing security gaps, not a generic framework.

We translate technical findings into investment decisions: what to tackle first, what provides the best risk reduction for your budget, and what can wait. The strategy distinguishes between quick wins and longer-term improvements.

Control Design & Implementation

SMB Engineering
Detailed guidance on designing security controls to address identified risks, with optional hands-on support during deployment.

Any control we design must be implementable and maintainable by you or your IT provider without ongoing dependency on us.

Expert at Hand

SMB Flexible Retainer
Have a security expert available whenever you need guidance.

Gain confidence in your risk assessments, security engineering decisions, and compliance strategies, knowing you have support to make informed choices and avoid costly mistakes.

Technical Security Review

Technical Threat-Modelling
Systematic security assessments using threat modeling and controls analysis to identify realistic attack paths and prioritize security investments.

We map identified risks directly to implementable security controls, providing practical guidance that fits your team's capabilities rather than overwhelming lists of theoretical vulnerabilities. Each assessment can be conducted individually or as part of a series, allowing you to start small and see results quickly.


About


Solais Security

Solais Security is an independent security consultancy run by Kevin Quill, based in the Republic of Ireland. I provide pragmatic security solutions and guidance for start-ups and SMEs, focusing on threat modelling, cloud architecture, control design, and developing control frameworks that align external compliance requirements with internal technologies and working practices.

Specific services include:

  • Threat Modelling & Risk Assessments
  • Cloud Security Architecture (AWS,GCP)
  • Security Control Design (Cloud, IAM, Vulnerability Managment, etc..)
  • Aligning Compliance Frameworks (ISO 27001, PCI, etc.) with your technical controls and processes
  • General Security Consultancy Retainer - Have a security expert at hand when needed

The Founder

He has a proven track record of delivering pragmatic, high-impact security improvements by designing scalable controls, automating compliance and monitoring, and partnering with engineering teams to balance risk reduction with business impact.

  • Acts as a force multiplier by guiding engineers, prioritising risk-based remediation, and enabling teams to make secure and pragmatic design decisions without slowing delivery
  • Communicates effectively across audiences, from engineers to senior leadership, tailoring technical and risk insights to drive alignment and investment
  • Builds bridges between Security and Engineering, fostering trust and collaboration to deliver outcomes with measurable business value
